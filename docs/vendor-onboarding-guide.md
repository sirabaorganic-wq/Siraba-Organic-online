# Vendor Onboarding Guide

## Overview
This guide outlines the process for vendors to onboard onto the Siraba Organic platform. The onboarding process is designed to collect necessary business information, verify vendor legitimacy, and set up their account for selling organic products.

## Prerequisites
Before starting the onboarding process, vendors should have:
- Valid business registration documents
- GST certificate (if applicable)
- FSSAI license (for food products)
- Organic certification documents
- Bank account details for payouts
- Business address and contact information

## Onboarding Steps

### Step 1: Business Details
Vendors need to provide comprehensive business information including:
- **Business Description**: A detailed description of the business, products offered, and unique selling points
- **Website**: Business website URL (optional)
- **GST Number**: GST registration number (required for Indian businesses)
- **PAN Number**: Permanent Account Number
- **FSSAI Number**: Food Safety and Standards Authority license number (required for food products)

### Step 2: Address Details
Complete business address information:
- **Street Address**: Full street address including building number
- **City**: City name
- **State**: State/Province
- **Postal Code**: PIN/ZIP code
- **Country**: Currently supports India

### Step 3: Bank Details
Banking information for commission payouts:
- **Account Holder Name**: Name as per bank records
- **Account Number**: Bank account number
- **Bank Name**: Full bank name
- **IFSC Code**: Indian Financial System Code
- **Branch Name**: Bank branch name
- **UPI ID**: Unified Payments Interface ID (optional)

### Step 4: Select Plan
Choose from available vendor plans:
- **Starter Plan**: Basic features for new vendors
- **Professional Plan**: Advanced features for growing businesses
- **Enterprise Plan**: Full-featured plan for established vendors

Plans can be billed monthly or annually with discounts for annual billing.

### Step 5: Document Upload
Upload required compliance documents:
- **Business License/Registration** (Required): Proof of business registration
- **GST Certificate**: GST registration certificate
- **FSSAI License**: Food safety license
- **Organic Certification**: Organic farming/product certification
- **PAN Card**: Permanent Account Number card

**Document Requirements:**
- Supported formats: PDF, JPEG, PNG, JPG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 5MB per document
- All documents must be clearly readable

## Verification Process
After completing all steps:
1. Documents are submitted for admin review
2. Business details are verified
3. Compliance documents are checked for authenticity
4. Account is activated upon approval
5. Vendor receives notification of approval status

## Post-Onboarding
Once approved, vendors can:
- Access vendor dashboard
- Add and manage products
- View orders and analytics
- Manage inventory
- Process payouts
- Communicate with customers

## Support
For assistance during onboarding:
- Contact support team
- Check documentation for help
- Review common issues and solutions

## Common Issues
- **Document Upload Failures**: Ensure files are under 5MB and in supported formats
- **GST Verification**: Ensure GST number is valid and matches business details
- **Bank Details**: Verify IFSC code and account details are correct
- **Incomplete Information**: All required fields must be filled before proceeding

## Timeline
- Document submission: Immediate
- Initial review: 1-2 business days
- Final approval: 2-5 business days (depending on document verification)
- Account activation: Within 24 hours of approval
